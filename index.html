About me

I'm a Mathematics and Statistics Co-op student at McMaster University with a strong interest in data analytics, AI, and business strategy. I enjoy using data to uncover insights, solve real-world problems and support data-driven decision-making. Through both professional and academic experience, I've developed skills in tools such as Python, R, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to analyze complex datasets and communicate insights effectively.

Outside of work and school, I enjoy travel, fashion, and hiking.
